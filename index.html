Executive Summary

SSD Consultant is pleased to present this comprehensive proposal for the development of a Membership Management Website. This platform will serve as a centralized digital solution for managing member registrations, subscriptions, payments, and administrative operations — modelled on best practices from similar platforms like agarwalsamajmadras.org.

This document outlines the complete scope of work, phase-wise delivery plan, technology stack, pricing, and terms governing this engagement. Our goal is to deliver a secure, scalable, and user-friendly membership portal within 15–30 working days.
